TRUST AND COMPLIANCE

Security and Accessibility

Security

  • Ed25519 digital signatures for all critical operations
  • x25519 E2E encryption on mesh (zero-knowledge relays)
  • Hash-chained audit log with tamper detection
  • Nonce replay protection on proof-of-delivery

Accessibility (WCAG)

  • High contrast dark theme: 19.4:1 ratio (exceeds AA 4.5:1)
  • Color-independent indicators: text labels alongside color
  • Semantic typography scale, 44px minimum touch targets
